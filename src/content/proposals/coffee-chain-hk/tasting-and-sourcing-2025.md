# Overview

This proposal outlines a comprehensive coffee sourcing and tasting program designed to elevate your coffee chain's offerings in Hong Kong through direct relationships with Brazilian producers and exclusive tasting events.

# Direct Sourcing Partnership

## Producer Network

We will connect you with three premium coffee estates in Brazil:

* **Fazenda Santa Clara** (Minas Gerais) - Specialty arabica, natural process
* **Sítio Boa Vista** (São Paulo) - Pulped natural, catuaí variety
* **Fazenda do Lobo** (Espírito Santo) - Experimental processing methods

## Quality Assurance

* Pre-shipment cupping samples
* Quality scoring (85+ SCA minimum)
* Traceability documentation
* Direct communication with producers

## Volume & Pricing

* **Minimum order:** 10 bags (60kg each)
* **Pricing:** Market rate minus 15% (direct relationship benefit)
* **Frequency:** Quarterly shipments
* **Contract term:** 12 months

# Tasting Events
